# Organization Reduction Summary

This document describes the reduction of organizations to a maximum of 6 per institution type.

## Rationale

To simplify the data structure and focus on the most important organizations in each sector, we reduced the number of organizations to a maximum of 6 per institution type (or 1 where there is naturally only one main organization).

## Final Statistics

- **Total institution types**: 17

- **Maximum organizations per type**: 6

## Benefits

1. **Simplified Structure**: Easier to navigate and understand
2. **Focus on Key Players**: Highlights the most important organizations in each sector
3. **Reduced Redundancy**: Less duplicate data
4. **Better Performance**: Fewer folders and files to manage
5. **Easier Maintenance**: Simpler to update and maintain

## Organizations Retained

The organizations retained were selected based on:
- Importance in the Zambian market
- Mentioned in original requirements
- Market share and visibility
- Government/regulatory importance
